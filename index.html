About the Tournament

The Free Fire Nepal Championship 2024 is the most anticipated esports event in Nepal, bringing together the best Free Fire players and teams from across the country. Compete for glory, fame, and a massive prize pool of Rs 4000. The tournament will feature intense battles, strategic gameplay, and unforgettable moments.

Dates: March 15 - March 20, 2024
Format: Group Stage, Quarterfinals, Semifinals, and Finals
Platform: Free Fire (Mobile)

Rules and Regulations

General Rules
  • All participants must be residents of Nepal.
  • Each team must consist of 4 players and 1 substitute.
  • Players must use their own devices and accounts.
  • Cheating or hacking will result in disqualification.
Gameplay Rules
  • Matches will be played in Classic Battle Royale mode.
  • Emulators are strictly prohibited.
  • Teams must adhere to the schedule.
  • Tiebreaker: Most kills advance.
Code of Conduct
  • Maintain sportsmanship and respect.
  • No harassment or toxic behavior.
  • Organizers' decisions are final.
